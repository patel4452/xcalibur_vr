Q:

Why choose Micro Credentials (MC)?

A:

UNITEN's MC's include fundamental topics, special interest titles and niche courses in energy and technology as well as generic MPU courses that can be transfer credited to any Malaysian university.

Interested learners are invited to enroll in UNITEN's Micro-credential modules to upgrade their knowledge and skill set, which requires minimal investment in time and cost. Learners completing a series of Micro-credential modules will be rewarded with digital badges, recognised by relevant accreditation bodies and industries.

Another benefit is that learners enrolled into UNITEN's MC modules have the opportunity to use the earned credentials to upgrade into UNITEN's academic programmes, either as full-time or part-time learners.

Q:

Why choose Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC)?

A:

UNITEN's MOOC's include fundamental topics, special interest titles and niche courses in energy and technology as well as generic MPU courses that can be transfer credited to any Malaysian university.

Learners completing an MOOC course will be rewarded with a digital badge, recognised by variuos accreditation bodies and industries.

Another benefit is that learners enrolled into UNITEN's MOOC courses can use earned credentials to upgrade into UNITEN's academic programmes, either as full-time or part-time learners.

Q:

Why choose Open Distance Learning (ODL)?

A:

These ODL programmes are suitable for busy working adults as well as younger adults who have recently graduated from matriculation or other preparatory programmes.

ODL students will benefit from a one-stop and user-friendly Learning Management System (LMS) which is accessible 24/7 from their mobile devices. The attractive and intuitive online portal of BRIGHTENX LMS includes study information and digital resourses. It is an environment conducive to learning where ODL students can interact with their instructors and fellow classmates.
