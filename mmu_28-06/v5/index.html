Build Your Own Curriculum at MMU!

Build your own curriculum is a concept to allow students to build curriculum in a guided and yet flexible way. In MMU, students can build their curriculum according to their interest and diversify their skill sets upon graduation. This enables graduates from MMU to fit into the diverse, challenging and demanding job market today. Students can stack up courses based on free elective slots from any Faculty as well as maximizing the free elective slot for a collective minor package offered by all Faculties.
