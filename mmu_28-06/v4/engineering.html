Extensive Industry Collaboration

MMU has strong collaborations with some of the biggest names in the global industry

MMU has long made a way of life of welcoming with open arms any institution and organization keen to collaborate. Whether they’re from academia, the government, or industry, MMU is always eager to explore new ways of entering mutually-beneficial arrangements. 

By being involved in innovation on so many fronts, MMU hopes to benefit its ultimate stakeholders and its students. Being innovators lead MMU academics into becoming better lecturers, not just at their respective subjects, but in teaching the students on how to become innovators themselves.

MMU has come to collaborate with some of the biggest names of the global industry, a veritable who’s who of the world, including ZTE, Nokia, Dell, Intel, Microsoft, Cisco and Motorola. Our work with them ensure that our students will become a new generation of innovators.
