Professor Dr. Abdul Mohaimin bin Noordin Ayus was born in Sitiawan Perak on 20th August 1951. He is currently with the Faculty of Law, Multimedia University, Melaka Campus, Melaka, Malaysia. He graduated with Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) (Hons.) degree in 1987, Master of Comparative Law (MCL) degree in 1989 and postgraduate Diploma in Shariah Law and Practice (DSLP) in 1995 from International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM).
