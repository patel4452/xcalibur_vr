Selecting the Right Vacuum Cleaner
for Your Household

LOREM IPSUM - Apri; 25, 2022 10:53 AM

Simply selecting the right vacuum cleaner for your household can make chores easier and quicker

A good vacuum cleaner can help reduce clean up time and add convenience to your life. However, your decision for a new vacuum cleaner cannot be based on price and aesthetics alone. The key is to find a vacuum cleaner that will suit your lifestyle and make vacuuming less of a chore. The first step is knowing the different types of vacuum cleaners, including the strengths of each.

Robotic vacuums

Robotic vacuums are automatic, smart machines that can help ease your cleaning routine.

Representing the latest in vacuum cleaning technology, robotic vacuums clean everything in their path. Being equipped with boundary sensors and cameras allow the robots to avoid obstacles or falling off steps. These smart machines automatically return to their base to dispose of collected dust and recharge, allowing for minimal user attention. Robotic vacuums are perfect for those who do not have the time to clean their house

Cannister vacuums

A powerful cleaning device that guarantees you effective results.

Cannister vacuums are known as the powerhouse of vacuum cleaners because of their ability to carry out heavy-duty cleaning. Cannister vacuums come in two parts – the nozzle-head that is flexible enough to reach tight corners and the cannister that holds a large capacity dust bin. If powerful cleaning performance is your top priority, then a cannister vacuum is the best option for you.

Handheld cordless vacuums

Handheld vacuums are portable, easy to use and great at cleaning crumbs and dust in places that are hard to reach.

Because of its ease of use, lightweight and quiet operating noise, a handheld vacuum is a handy tool to use around the house. It is portable and small enough to reach tight or uneven spaces, and the assortment of vacuum heads makes it suitable to clean areas such as inside your car or furniture. You may not be using this to clean your floors, but it works great in picking up crumbs and dust and cleaning small messes.

Stick vacuums

This lightweight and cordless device makes vacuuming your house a lot more fun and easier.

Also known as sweepers, many modern stick vacuums are designed to be lightweight and cordless, making it easy to carry the device up and downstairs and clean high, hard-to-reach places. These vacuum cleaners are now increasing in popularity as newer, more powerful stick vacuums are being introduced.

Choosing between a bag vs bagless feature

Bagged vacuum cleaners are more suitable for those suffering from dust allergies as they are more hygienic and mess-free.

Bagged vacuum cleaners are the more hygienic option as dust, debris and other contaminants are locked inside airtight bags. Once the bags are full, removing and disposing of the bags are hassle- and mess-free, perfect for those who suffer from allergies.

Bagless vacuum cleaners, however, are often cheaper and have a larger capacity than bagged alternatives, while also being more environmentally friendly as you do not need to replace filled dust bags.

Understanding the different types of vacuum cleaners and how they work can make choosing the right vacuum cleaner for your household a lot easier

The smart independent worker

Your home may have a variety of different types of flooring. Because of this, you must ensure your cleaning tool suits your home. The newest model from iRobot, the Roomba® J7+, is a robotic vacuum cleaner that is equipped with dual multi-surface rubber brushes. The pliability of the brushes allows the robot to work its magic on carpeted floors, tiles and of course, hardwood floors. Its ‘no-bristle’ feature also means it works well in picking up pet fur as there are no bristles for the hair to get tangled on. Its PrecisionVision Navigation feature allows it to move around the house while avoiding cables, cords and even pet waste.

The dual-purpose master

Nobody wants to lug around a chunky or heavy vacuum cleaner around the house. Among the biggest plus points of the Corvan 2-in-1 K18 Cordless Vacuum + Mop is its handleability. It weighs approximately 1.5kg and its ultra-slim body makes it useful in getting into pesky corners between heavy furniture. The most notable feature is its ability to mop and vacuum your house at one go, saving precious time. In addition, the one-hand dirt disposal mechanism also makes disposing of waste a mess-free event.

The perfect cleaning partner

Besides handling, you’d want to set your sights on a vacuum cleaner that is easy to maintain in the long run. Panasonic, has a range of sturdy vacuum cleaners that are also fully washable, meaning you will have an easier time washing away piled up gunk. For those who have concerns with allergies, a range of Panasonic vacuum models comes with highly functional HEPA filters that work wonders in trapping dust mites, pet dander, and other allergens from escaping. Now that you’ve got the lowdown on what to consider when selecting the right vacuum cleaner for your household, you can look forward to knowing vacuuming would no longer be a daunting task!
