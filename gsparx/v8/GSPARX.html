Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Solar powering your home goes a long way. We have a compiled some questions that may address your concerns. Feel free to clarify with us more if needed upon enquiry.

Who is GSPARX and what do we offer?

GSPARX is a wholly owned subsidiary of TNB that focuses on green retail generation business. At the moment, we provide end-to-end solution from design, supply, install, testing and commissioning of rooftop solar PV solution for all customer segments from residential to commercial, industrial and government sectors.

How much TNB bill that I can reduce after installing solar PV system?

Under ideal condition, the solar PV system can reduce up to 90% of your electricity bill subject to the solar capacity installed.

What is inclusive in GSPARX standard package for residential customers?

Our standard package inclusive of the followings:

  • Design, Supply, Install, Testing & Commissioning of Solar PV System come with the balance of system
  • Standard mounting structure
  • Online Monitoring System
  • Solar Insurance
  • NEM application with SEDA and TNB NEM meter application
  • 2 years free maintenance package
  • Equipment and workmanship warranties
What is Net Energy Metering Scheme (“NEM”)?

Our solar package includes the application of NEM. NEM is an incentive scheme introduced by the government that allows customers to install solar PV system on their roof and enable the energy generated from the solarto be used first. If there is an excess of energy generated by solar system, it will automatically flow to TNB grid where you can offset your electricity bill to further reduce your bill.

Is there any quota or expiry date for the NEM scheme?

Yes. The NEM scheme comes with quota which is manage by the Sustainable Energy Development Authority (SEDA). The current NEM 3.0 offer will expire on 31st December 2023 and the quota availability is based on first come first serve basis. You can read more about the NEM scheme and the quota availability at http://www.seda.gov.my/.

What payment option do you offer for customers?
  • Cash Purchase- pay lump sum amount
  • Credit Card- We have a special instalment plan with selected banks for 12,24 and 36 months.
  • Personal Loan- Finance the PV system via personal loan. Choose your preferred bank. Special rate by Bank Islam for GLC employees.
  • Customer can also choose to put a down payment to reduce the credit card or personal loan installment.
Can my roof structure support the solar PV system?

We will schedule a site assessment to your house to study on the roof structure of your house. If your roof is not feasible to install solar PV system, we will proceed to refund the deposit to you.

How much energy can be generated by the solar PV system?

The energy generated by the solar PV system varies based on the weather, system capacity and surrounding factors such as shading. In Malaysia, we normally use average 3.5 peak sun hour in calculating the energy generated by the solar PV system.

Does your package include battery solution?

No for now, because at the moment the cost of the battery is high making it not commercially viable to install solar PV with battery. We are closely monitoring the price and as a GSPARX customer, you will be amongst the first group of customers to own the battery when it is commercially viable soon.

Moreover, with NEM that allows you to sell excess energy to TNB and be paid at the same rate as you buy from TNB (NEM Rakyat), TNB becomes your ‘battery’ and you save the investment on the storage unit.
