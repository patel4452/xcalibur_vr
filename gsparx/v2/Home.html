How can I install solar PV system for my house?

Take these simple steps to own your Solar PV system:

  • Register your interest and key in your detail at our website www.gsparx.com or at this link: http://bit.ly/gsparxcustomerservice

  • Our team will arrange for installation in which we will finalized the size based on the site assessment
