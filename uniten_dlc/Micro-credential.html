What is Micro-Credentials

Micro-credential (MC) offers a more dynamic, competitive, demand-driven, shorter and less expensive alternative to the traditional degree. As their name implies, MCs focus on much smaller modules of learning than those covered in conventional academic awards, which often allow learners to complete the required work over a shorter period (MQA, 2019).

In their most developed form, MCs represent more than mere recognition of smaller modules of learning. They form part of a digital credentialing ecosystem, made possible by digital communications technologies, establishing networks of interest through which people can share information about what a learner knows and can do (Milligan and Kennedy, in James et al., 2017).

Interested learners may take UNITEN micro-credential modules to upgrade their knowledge and skill set, with minimal investment in time and cost. Completion of a series of micro-credential modules will be rewarded with a digital badge that is recognised by accreditation bodies and the industry.

- Describe the strategies and tools in digital marketing
- Explain micro and macro environment of digital marketing

  • To enable learners to explain micro and macro environment of digital marketing

COURSE OUTCOMES

  • CO1 – Identify the concepts and terms related to digital marketing (C1)

Description

In this course, you will be able to explore the world of three-dimensional system: the graphs and the operations within! From the basic knowledge in the geometry of space to the applications in motions such as velocity, acceleration etc. using vector-valued functions.

For anyone who have passed pre-University calculus are welcome to join this course. If you are a mathematics lover, or interested in engineering, you are invited to join this course as well to expand your knowledge borders in mathematical field.

wHAT WILL YOU LEARN

At the end of this course, you should be able to identify equations of lines, planes, curves on plane, cylinders and quadric surfaces, perform calculation on vectors and calculation involving lines and planes, differentiate and integrate vector functions, find the motion related vector functions and the motion related coefficients.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

The course aims to guide the learners to understand, distinguish and perform calculations involving vectors in three-dimensional system, to visualize a problem and solution in three-dimensional system, to use vectors to solve problems in three- dimensional system, and to apply and extend the basic concepts of differential calculus to vector-valued functions.

COURSE OUTCOMES

  • identify equations of lines, planes and curve on plane, 
  • perform calculation on vectors, 
  • perform calculation involving lines and planes, 
  • identify cylinders and quadric surfaces, 
  • differentiate vector functions, 
  • integrate vector functions, 
  • find the motion related vector functions, and 
  • calculate the motion related coefficients.
