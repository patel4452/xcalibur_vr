It all begins here
EduCity Iskandar is Asia’s first multi-campus education city with 305-acres of universities, higher education institutions, research & development centres, EduCity Village, EduCity Hub, EduCity Sport Complex as well as recreational facilities. The education institutions in this education city are made out of esteemed and accredited educational institutions locally and internationally. All education institutions have equal access to quality shared services and facilities, such as student accommodations, security, recreational and sports facilities. This has truly made EduCity Iskandar the choice education city and the first of its kind in Asia.
EduCity Iskandar Malaysia is a fully integrated education city recognized as an EntryPoint Project (EPP) under the National Key Economic Area (NKEA), which will play an integral part in the success of Malaysia’s Economic Transformation Programme. A prime destination for world-class education, EduCity Iskandar is easily accessible via the Coastal Highway that links Medini to Johor Bahru city centre.

Iskandar Investment

AA subsidiary of Iskandar Investment Berhad (IIB), we are driven by our parent company who is the key player in transforming Iskandar Malaysia into a regional metropolis of international standing. Incorporated in November 2006, IIB is tasked to ensure Iskandar Malaysia continues its successful trajectory into an attractive investment destination and a vibrant liveable region via catalytic and high impact projects.

IIB focuses on the development of Iskandar Puteri in the education, creative, tourism and leisure, as well as health and wellness sectors. IIB’s prominent accomplishments include the successful joint ventures and investment partnerships in LEGOLAND® Malaysia Resort, Iskandar Puteri, Medini and infrastructure projects under the 9th Malaysia Plan in Iskandar Malaysia.

Iskandar Puteri

IIskandar Puteri is the largest urban integrated development in South East Asia, comprising 24,000 acres of signature catalytic developments aligned with identified sectors and industries in the 10th Malaysia Plan.

“ “The best-in-class faculties in EduCity Iskandar are expected to be a feeder of talents to support Malaysia" ”
- EduCity Iskandar Malaysia chairman Datuk Khairil Anwar Ahmad

Formerly known as Nusajaya, it was launched in 2007 as one of five flagship zones of Iskandar Malaysia — the country’s foremost emerging economic zone. Iskandar Puteri’s strategic location and close proximity to Singapore has been proven to spur the economic growth of the locality.

• Strategically located at the crossroads of the East-West trade lanes

• Two international airports

• Five seaports keep the city within minutes from Singapore and a few hours by air from Asia’s economic powerhouses

Iskandar Puteri was planned with sustainability as a priority and is envisioned to become Malaysia’s most connected and liveable city by 2025.

IP is rapidly becoming the region’s culture and entertainment hub due to its affinity for eclectic happenings and public art projects. At least 34 public art installations and 138 cultural events took place within IP in conjunction with Iskandar Malaysia’s 10th anniversary last year, attracting droves of visitors and talents from the ASEAN region.

Though IP accounts for a mere 4% of Iskandar Malaysia’s total area, it is one of the nation’s most-recognised developments. IP scored global attention in 2012 by obtaining the Best Masterplan Award at the FIABCI Prix d’Excellence Awards. On top of setting the benchmark for city safety and security planning, IP is also home to Kota Iskandar, Johor’s administrative centre.
