OUM's Payment Options*

*Terms & conditions apply

PTPTN

→ Get up to 100% loan if you’re 45 years and below at the time of application.

→ Repay your loan in instalments only starting 6 months after you’ve graduated.

→ PTPTN interest is only a flat rate of 1% per annum.

EPF

→ Working adults can withdraw up to 100% from their second account to finance their education.

→ Withdrawal to finance your/your spouse’s/your children’s tuition fees

→ Joint withdrawal with your spouse to finance your/ your spouse’s/ your children’s tuition fees

→ When can you withdraw: Every semester or academic year

Financial Aid

→ 50% discount for senior citizens aged 60 and above, and physically challenged learners (OKU)

→ Bursary for students with outstanding performance in their studies

→ Loans available under partnership with Affin Bank

→ A tax relief of up to RM7,000 is claimable if you’re paying for your own further education.

Self Pay

→ Enjoy flexible monthly payment options with 0% interest.

Tax Exemptions

→ A tax relief of up to RM7,000 is claimable if you’re paying for your own further education

→ Any courses of study taken on for upskilling or self-enhancement purposes are claimable, but only up to RM1,000.

Graduate Faster Using Your Work Experience

Find out how to leverage on your prior experience to shorten your study duration through Accreditation of Prior Experiential Learning (APEL).

 Explore APEL
  
  • APEL A
  • APEL C

If you have work experience but lack academic qualifications, APEL (A) will enable you to enrol in a programme based on assessment of the knowledge you have gained through previous working experience.

Through APEL C, experiential learning gained through informal or non-formal means are assessed against specified course learning outcomes for learners to obtain APEL credits. With these credits, learners are given exceptions from sitting certain courses which will help them to complete their studies faster.
