Get to Know​ Labour and Delivery at SJMC

Discover the differences between normal delivery and a c-section ​​before you decide on how to deliver your little one.

Normal Delivery

This is the most common and safe option for delivery. You may opt for painkillers like an epidural, or you may go medication-free.

Benefits of delivering your baby normally: 

  • Lower risk of infection for you and baby
  • Shorter hospital stay
  • Quicker recovery and return to activities
  • Higher rate of breastfeeding initiation
  • Fewer anesthesia complications
Caeserean Section

A caesarean section, or c-section is a fast and safe procedure done when a normal delivery is not possible or safe for you and the baby.

Benefits of delivering your baby via c-section: 

  • Avoid prolonged labour or pushing
  • Scheduled delivery timing
  • Decreased infant mortality
  • Lower risk of blood transfusion
  • Decrease urinary incontinence
